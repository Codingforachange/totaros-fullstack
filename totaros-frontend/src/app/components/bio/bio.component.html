Our Story

Passed down through generations, Totaro's & Sons represents the heart of authentic Italian street food. What started in our family kitchen as a collection of hand-written recipes has evolved into a fully mobile culinary experience.

Every sauce is simmered for hours, every cutlet is hand-breaded, and every meal is built with the same passion and premium ingredients we serve at our own family table.
